== Baptism Birthplace Records Wrong?

Are some baptism's birthplaces wrong?

In baptism entries for two of the children of Carl Friedrich Gottlieb
Krückeberg we have two different birthplaces given--or are these no
birthplaces but current residences?  The birthplace for Carl Friedrich
born in 1807 and the daughter born the next year is given only as
"Berenbusch". In these records, there is a column for the "Name, Stand,
Religion and Birthplace of the Father". However, for the 1807 birth of
Carl Friedrich, this column contains "Carl Friedrich Gottlieb Krückeberg.
Schneider u. Einlieger in n. 14 in Bärenbusch. Luth. Conf.", while for the
the 1812 birth of daughter Carloline Dorothee Krückeberg it has "Carl
Friedrich Gottlieb Krückeberg n. 18 in Bärenbusch Luth. Conf.".

So is "n. 14 Berenbsuch" and "n. 18 Berenbusch" the address at the time
the child was born despite the column stating it is the "birthplace of the
father"? The actual birth record of Carl Friedrich Gottlieb is of no help
in answering this question since it only mentions Berenbusch.  

Answer: The answer is given in the column "Wohnort der Eltern", which
has the same address as that of the father.
