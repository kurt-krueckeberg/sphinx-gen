# Wallensen Kirchenbücher

This section contains records from the Wallensen parish, which included the village of
[Thüste where Heinrich August Friedrich Christian Krückberg from the village of](https://www.meyersgaz.org/place/20951047)[Thüste was born.  He and his wife would later immigrate and settle in Adams
township, Allen county, Indiana, and become part of the](https://www.meyersgaz.org/place/20951047)[Söst-Emmanuel congregation.](http://www.emmanuelsoest.org/our-history/)
