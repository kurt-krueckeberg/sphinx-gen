# 1836 Sophie Wilhelmine Louise Buuck Baptism and Birth

In 1836 Sophie Wilhelmine Louise Buuck, daughter of Ernst Diedrich Buuck and Christine Wilhelmine Döhrmann, was
born at Windheim 78.
