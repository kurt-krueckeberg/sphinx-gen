# Notices of Emigration from Schaumburg-Lippe

Prospective emigrants from Schaumburg-Lippe were made public through
**official notices** in the principality’s official periodical, not by
private newspaper ads. The purpose was to summon possible creditors or
other claimants before the person left. In 1849, those notices would have
appeared in the current issues of ***Anzeigen des Fürstenthums
Schaumburg-Lippe*** (**Notices of the Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe**).

The surviving 1849 issues were later bound together as the annual volume
***Anzeigen des Fürstenthums Schaumburg-Lippe*** (**Notices of the
Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe**). **1849**. That bound volume has now
been digitized by the Bayerische Staatsbibliothek / Münchener
DigitalisierungsZentrum and is available online here:

- [Anzeigen des Fürstenthums Schaumburg-Lippe. 1850 (Official Gazette / Notices of the Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe), 1850.](https://www.digitale-sammlungen.de/en/details/bsb10002333)
