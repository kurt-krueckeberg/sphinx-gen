# 25 August 1849 Public Notice of Emigration of Heinrich Wilhelm Weiland to America

In many German states, emigrants were officially published before they
departed so creditors could bring outstanding claims before the local
authorities. In Schaumburg-Lippe, these notices appeared in the [Anzeigen
des Fürstenthums Schaumburg-Lippe (Notices of the Principality of
Schaumburg-Lippe)](https://www.digitale-sammlungen.de/en/details/bsb10002333),
the principality’s official publication for government notices, orders, and
announcements. They were especially important when an emigrant had sold
property before he left for America, because sale proceed could be used to
satisfy outstanding claims.

## Heinrich Wilhelm Weiland's Emigration Officially Published

The notice of Heinrich Wilhelm Weiland's planned emigration to America
appeared in the [August 25,
1849](https://www.digitale-sammlungen.de/en/view/bsb10002333?page=223),
edition of Notices of the Principality of Schaumburg-Lippe.

The section header **Bekanntmachung** (Notice) appears in the left column
of page 204. The notice of Heinrich Wilhelm Weilands's sale of his Stätte
(holding) prior to his family's emigration to America appears in the right
column.

## Transliteration

```{div} doc-text
Bückeburg. Der Colon Weiland No 20 in Evesen, welcher nach Amerika
auszuwandern im Begriff steht, hat seine Stätte an den Colon Wöbling No 2
daselbst verkauft und werden auf An- trag des erstern alle diejenigen,
welche Forderungen an ihn haben, hierdurch aufgefordert, zur Anmeldung
derselben

am 5. k. M. September

Morgens 11 Uhr vor hiesigem Amte zu erscheinen und Befriedigung zu gewärtigen.

Bückeburg, den 23. August 1849.

Fürstlich Schaumb. Lippisches Amt.

Höcker. ᐧ König.
```

## Translation

```{div} doc-text
Bückeburg. The Colon Weiland, No. 20 in Evesen, who is about to emigrate to
America, has sold his holding to the colonist Wöbling, No. 2 there.

At the request of the former, all those who have claims against him are
hereby summoned to appear before the local Amt on the 5th of next month,
September, at 11 o’clock in the morning, to register those claims and to
expect settlement.

Bückeburg, 23 August 1849.

Princely Schaumburg-Lippe Amt.

Höcker.        König.
```
