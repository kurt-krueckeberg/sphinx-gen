# 1694 Johann Heinrich Vahlsing Baptism

## Citation

The baptism of Johann Heinrich Vahlsing occurred on 29 April 1694. It
appears on {ref}`image 67, entry #15 <FRI-B-1694a>` in the Frille "Verzeichnis
der Getauften, Getrauten, Gestorbenen (Register of Baptism, Marriages,
Deaths) 1664-1757.

## Transliteration and Translation

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

15 Dom: Jub: den 29 Apr: hat der Schäfer in Papinghausen, Johann
Vahlsing, seines Söhnlein Johann Heinrich taufen lassen
```

Translation

```{code-block} text

15. On Jubilate Sunday, the 29th of April, the shepherd in Papinghausen,
Johann Vahlsing, had his little son Johann Heinrich baptized.
```
