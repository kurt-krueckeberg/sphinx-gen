# 1742 Ann Maria Elisabeth Schäkel Baptism

## Record

The baptism of Ann Maria Elisabeth Schäkel occurred on 9 October 1742. It

Frille "Verzeichnis der Getauften, Getrauten, Gestorbenen (Register of

## Transliteration and Translation

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

45.) d 9ten Oct. Anna Maria Elisabeth Schäkels. P. Friederich Wilhelm
Schäkel. M. Anna Catherina Vahlsings beÿde aus Päpinghausen. S. Ilse
Margarethe Vahlsings, der Kindbetterin Bruders Tochter aus Päpinghausen
```

Translation

```{code-block} text

45.) On the 9th of October. Anna Maria Elisabeth Schäkel.
F. Friederich Wilhelm Schäkel.
M. Anna Catherina Vahlsing, both from Päpinghausen.
S. Ilse Margarethe Vahlsing, the daughter of the mother’s brother, from Päpinghausen.
```
