## Citation

On 12 October 1790, Jobst Heinrich Krückeberg, a cobbler from Berenbusch No
10, married Philippine Christine Eleonore Tegtmeyern from Meissen No 4.
The marriage is recorded on the [5 image, page 5, entry 13](PET-M-1790c)

The transliteration of the header for page 5 and marriage entries 8, 10 and 13 are below:

```{note}
1. The + sign denotes a death date. The **+1849** above the name of *Philippine Christine Eleonore Tegtmeyern* below
indicates she died in 1849.
2. The abbreviation **Jfr** stands for Jungfrau or maiden.
```

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

13) d 12th October ist copulirt Jobst Heinrich Kruckeberg aus Berenbusch
                                   gest. 21.10.22
    No 10 ein Schuster mit Jfr Philippine Christine Eleonore
        +1849
    Tegtmeyern aus Meissen[faded, ambiguous] N:41
```

```{note}
The + or cross sign denotes a death date. The apparent death date of Jobst Heinrich
is denoted with the preceding gest., meaning **gestorben** (died).
```
