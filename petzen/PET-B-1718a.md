Transliteration

```{div} doc-text
23 Eodem ist Henirch Bleeck aus Evesen Töchterlein Anna Ilhabe getauft\
```

Translation

```{div} doc-text
23 On the same day [9th October] was Henirch Bleeck[e]'s daughter from Evesen Anna Ilhabe getauft

Bleeck from Meißen.
```
