## Transliteration

```{div} doc-text
On 7 February, Hans Hermann Weÿland of Berenbusch’s little daughter,
begotten in fornication with Ilse Maria Vahlsing of Evesen, was baptized
[and named] Margaretha Elisabeth. Godparent: Bleecke’s eldest daughter.
```
