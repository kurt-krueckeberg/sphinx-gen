```{important}
It is very probable that **Jobst** is incorrect, a scribal error, or the use
of **Tochter (daughter)** meant "family daughter” in the social sense. Jobst
Heinrich Krückeberg did live at no. 10 Berenbusch, but he was born in 1765,
and thus only age 36 when Sophie Marie died in her 60th year.
No. 10 Berenbusch was also the home of Johann Heinrich Krückeberg, Jobst
Heinrich’s father.

The burial entry names Jobst Heinrich Krückeberg as the household reference
person (nachgelassen), indicating that Sophie Marie belonged to his
household at the time of her death. She was not a biological daughter of
Jobst Heinrich, who was 25 years her junior. *Tochter* then can be best
understood in a social/household sense. Her biological father was almost
certainly Johann Heinrich Krückeberg (†1759).
```
