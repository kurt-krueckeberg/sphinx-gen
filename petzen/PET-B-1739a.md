## Transliteration and Translation

Transliteration

```{code-block} text

11 hÿ Krückeberg [in] [B. B.] ein Söhnl[ein] tauft
lassen Johann Wilhelm [benamst?] [uncertain]
[seina?] Gevatter Valsing
```

:::{admonition} Explanations

```{important}
- **Berenbusch** is not mentioned explicitly. Likely
berenbusch abbreviation in the image is an abbreviation for it.
```

```{note}
**hÿ:** abbreviation of Latin **hujus (mensis)**, “of this month” (i.e., “11th of this same month”).
```

:::

Translation

```{code-block} text

On the 11th of this month [February] the Berenbusch Kruckeberg's little son was
baptized [named] Johann Wilhelm [uncertain]
His godfather Valsing
```
