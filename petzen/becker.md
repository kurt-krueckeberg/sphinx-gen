# Beckers in Petzen

These transliterations and translations of the Becker records found in the Petzen church registers
are based on the online the digitized versions of the [Petzen
Kirchenbücher](https://www.archion.de/de/alle-archive/niedersachsen/niedersaechsisches-landesarchiv/kirchenbuecher-der-evangelisch-lutherischen-landeskirche-schaumburg-lippe/petzen) found on [Archion.de](https://archion.de).
