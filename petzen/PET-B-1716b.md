# 1716 Johann Wilhelm Vahlsing Baptism

## Record

The baptism of Johann Wilhelm Vahlsing is on {ref}`image 124 <PET-B-1716b>`,
left-hand page, 3nd entry from the top, of the Petzen "Verzeichnis der
Getauften und Konfirmierten 1641-1784".

```{figure} images/petzen-band1a-img124.png
:class: image-override
```

## Transliteration

```{div} doc-text
d. 25t Novembr: ist Arend Henrich Vahlsings Schäffers auf der Höckersau
Söhnlein Johann Wilhelm getaufft gev. H. Verwalter Becker auß Nordholtz
```

## Translation

```{div} doc-text
On 25 November, Johann Wilhelm, the little son of Arend Henrich Vahlsing,
shepherd at Höckersau, was baptized. The godfather was Herr Becker,
the administrator/steward from Nordholz.
```
